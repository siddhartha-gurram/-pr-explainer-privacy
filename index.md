# PR Explainer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

## Data Collection
PR Explainer does not collect, store, or 
transmit any personal data.

## How It Works
When you click "Explain PR", the extension 
reads the Pull Request diff from the current 
GitHub page and opens claude.ai in a new tab 
with the content as text only.

## What We Don't Do
- We do not collect any personal information
- We do not track your browsing activity
- We do not store any data on our servers
- We do not sell data to third parties
